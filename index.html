Highly Qualified Health Psychologist

Dr Dayyanah Sumodhee is a Chartered Health Psychologist registered with the BPS and the HCPC. She is a highly qualified Health Psychologist with 7 years of experience in helping people with addictive health-damaging habits such as smoking or who have health conditions (respiratory, oncology, pain). She uses the most effective and scientific-based approach to provide the best psychological treatment. She uses various therapeutic tools such as behaviour change techniques, CBT, and mindfulness.

She offers therapy sessions over the phone or on Skype in English or in French.
